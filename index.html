JACKY LUI

About

Jacky Lui is an Australian lawyer specialised in resolving disputes in the energy, mining and technology sectors. He has worked with clients including governments and companies. Notably, he has successfully defended governments in cases involving the environment and sustainable development.

Owing to an interest in emerging technology, Jacky developed programming skills in Python and is adept in data analytics, machine learning and building AI models. He is also knowledgable in front-end web development in React, Next.js and Tailwind CSS.

He received a Bachelor of Laws and Bachelor of Commerce from UNSW Sydney, and completed a Masters of Laws at Humboldt University of Berlin.
