About

Jacky is an international lawyer with experience practising law across international law firms such as Herbert Smith Freehills Kramer, Taylor Wessing and Noerr. He's advised corporations and governments particularly in issues of public international law, international investment law, international tax, commercial and investor-state arbitration.

Jacky has worked on complex international disputes involving projects in the energy, mining and technology sectors. He has published and spoken on panels on various topics, including international investment law, mining and energy sectors and the environment, cybersecurity and blockchain technology.

Owing to an interest in emerging technology, he obtained programming skills in Python and am skilled with data analytics, machine learning and building AI models.

He holds an LL.M. from Humboldt University of Berlin, and Bachelors of Commerce and Law (LLB) with UNSW, Sydney.
